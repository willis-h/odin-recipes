Lasagna

Description

If you don't know what lasagna is, you probably don't have any business cooking it.

Ingredients

  • Dry lasagna
  • Bolognese sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Place one layer of dry lasagna into a pyrex dish
  2. Spread a layer of sauce on top of the dry lasagna
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 until pish is full
  4. Top dish with shredded cheese and cook in the oven at 475 for about 45 minutes.
  5. Throw everything in the trash and order McDonald's
